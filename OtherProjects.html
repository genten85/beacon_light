AMP (Action Mapping Project) collaborates with public and private organizations to promote a positive impact on youth and the community in Pierce County. AMP promotes participatory GIS where youth map their community. The data and information collected is used for analysis, sharing, and collaboration in a manner that promotes policychanges. More information can be found here!

My work with AMP is based on a separate funding stream, which involves collaboration with United Way of Pierce County. My responsibilities are:

1) To collect data involving United Way of Piece County, based on the following categories: approved agencies, strong families, and community impact partners.

2) Collect relevant Census data to a) generate a spatial layer of ALICE households at the block group scale for Pierce County, then b) convert the data in a manner that can be presented in zip code scale.

3) Create a set of maps that reflect relevant information for stakeholders and donors to read (Vol of 211 Calls per ALICE family count, percentage of ALICE families, and median family incomes).
