Greetings!


I am Kevin. I am a recent graduate from the University of Washington Tacoma with a Masters of Science in Geospatial Technologies. I earned my Bachelors in Social Welfare also from UW Tacoma. I am interested integrating GIS with contemporary social work to strengthen community organizations and improve local communities. There are various positive applications of GIS in social work, and trends in technological advancements would suggest it is beneficial for community organizations to improve their information infrasturcture to meet organizational needs.

As a persistent and perceptive cartographer, I provide products of desirable qualities and insight that benefits the interests of communities and organizations. I strive to build upon the connections I have with the clients I serve who seek my assistance. My promotion of integrity and equity is integral in ensuring these communities and organizations succeed and optimize on that success.
