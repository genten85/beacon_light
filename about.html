Greetings!


I am Kevin. I am a current grad student of the University of Washington Tacoma, pursuing a Masters in Geospatial Technologies. I earned my Bachelors in Social Welfare also from UW Tacoma. My interest with GIS is integrating it with social work as a way to strengthen community organizations to indirectly improve local communities. There are various positive applications of GIS in social work, and trends in technological advancements would suggest it is beneficial for community organizations to upgrade their information infrasturcture to meet contemporary needs.

As a persistent and perceptive cartographer, I provide products of desirable qualities and insight that benefits the interests of communities and organizations. I strive to build upon the connections I have with the clients I serve who seek my assistance. My promotion of integrity and equity is integral in ensuring these communities and organizations succeed and optimize on that success.
